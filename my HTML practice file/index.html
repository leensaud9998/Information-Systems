IS major in general

IS (Information Systems) major is ideal for students with an intrest in computer science, business, and information technology. The major is the perfect place for you if you are passionate about using technology for positive gains across society, both economic and humanistic.

Things to expect when studying IS:

  • you will learn how to code ALOT!
  • Learn about all the coding languges and the diffrence between them

What can I do with an IS degree?

  1. enable platforms like NETflix and Twitch to make better recommendations
